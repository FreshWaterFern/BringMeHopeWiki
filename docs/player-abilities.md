# Player abilities

This page covers your moment-to-moment toolkit: the core actions you always have, the
kick-off move, and the skills you unlock as you level up. Every action can be rebound in
the controls menu, so the names below refer to the in-game action rather than a specific
button.

## Core actions

- **Dash.** Your main burst of movement and the foundation for several other abilities.
  Many skills build on top of it.
- **Light attack and Heavy attack.** Your melee combo. Light attacks are quick, heavy
  attacks are slower and stronger and chain into a combo.
- **Parry.** Press the **Heavy attack** button right before an attack or projectile hits
  you to parry it. It works against any attack or projectile.
- **Grapple (grab and throw).** Grab an enemy and throw them. A thrown enemy collides
  with and hits other enemies along the way. Bosses and enemies that do not move cannot
  be grabbed.

## Kick Off: dashing off walls and enemies

Once you have the **Kick Off** skill, dashing into a wall or an enemy no longer stops
you. Instead you **kick off** of it and launch straight into a fresh dash.

- You shoot off in the direction you are aiming, at a strong speed boost (around 1.7x a
  normal dash) with a brief freeze on impact for weight.
- Kicking off an **enemy** also briefly freezes that enemy in place, so it doubles as a
  way to interrupt them.
- You can **chain** kick-offs across different walls and enemies to keep your momentum
  going and cross a room in a few hops.
- You cannot kick off the **same** target twice in a row. The game remembers the last
  thing you kicked off for a moment, so you need a different wall or enemy to keep the
  chain going.

Kick Off pairs well with anything that raises your dash speed, and with the fluid dash
skills below for crossing hazards at full momentum.

## Skills

Skills unlock automatically as your character level rises. The descriptions below are the
in-game skill text.

| Skill | What it does | Unlocks at |
|---|---|---|
| **Crushing Blow** | Adds a final, third hit to your heavy combo. | Level 1 |
| **Empowered Dash** | Fluids can be dashed across infinitely, so your dash no longer fizzles on them. | Level 2 |
| **Running Start** | Start a melee combo by punching during a dash. Works with both the light and heavy punches. | Level 3 |
| **Safe Dash** | While dashing you are immune to fluid effects such as poison and water. | Level 4 |
| **Kick Off** | Use walls and enemies to perform extra consecutive dashes and gain a speed boost. | Level 5 |
| **Great Leap** | Increased leap distance lets you cross larger gaps. | Level 10 |

**Safe Dash** and **Empowered Dash** both interact with [Fluids](fluids.md): Safe Dash
stops fluids from affecting you while you dash, and Empowered Dash stops fluids from
cutting your dash short.

## Notes

- The unlock levels above are character level milestones. You learn each skill the first
  time you reach its level.
- Many items change how these abilities feel, for example by boosting dash speed or
  adding effects to your punches. See the [Items](items/index.md) for those.
